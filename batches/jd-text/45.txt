About The Role
We're building the digital infrastructure that connects modern manufacturing companies with top-tier factories across the globe. As one of the first senior engineers joining a small, hands-on team, you'll have a direct impact on architecture decisions, product direction, and how we scale a complex marketplace platform.
You'll work day-to-day with our hands-on CTO and a lead-level engineer - meaning you need to be comfortable owning problems end-to-end, from data model to deployment, without a large team to hand things off to.
If you have strong TypeScript instincts, care about building systems that are both correct and maintainable, and are energised (not intimidated) by the full stack - we'd love to talk.
What You'll Do
Build and maintain full-stack features using Next.js, React, and TypeScript - from server components to client-side interactions
Design and query a PostgreSQL database via Prisma, with an eye for performance and data integrity
Style UIs with TailwindCSS, working closely with design intent rather than just implementing specs
Write meaningful tests - unit, integration, and end-to-end where it counts
Build and maintain infrastructure across our hybrid cloud setup (AWS, Hetzner bare metal, Cloudflare) using an IaC approach
Contribute to architectural decisions, including our event-driven and microservices patterns
Deploy and operate services - you'll own what you ship
What You'll Bring
4+ years of professional experience with a meaningful portion in full-stack or senior roles
Strong command of TypeScript and React, including Next.js in production
Solid backend fundamentals - you're equally comfortable in a Prisma schema and a complex SQL query
Experience with PostgreSQL in a production environment
Comfort working across the stack as a generalist - you don't wait for someone else to own the infrastructure or the data model
Hands-on experience with Docker and container-based deployments
Familiarity with IaC tooling (Terraform) and cloud environments, particularly AWS
Exposure to event-driven architectures or microservices patterns is a plus
Familiarity with state management approaches (Redux Toolkit, Tanstack Query, Zustand)
Preferably based in the Netherlands (alternatively in Turkiye or Poland)
What's In It For You
Fully remote position
MacBook Pro provided
Learning budget to support your professional growth
20 days paid leave plus Dutch public holidays
Discretionary bonus potential
A small, senior team - no bureaucracy, high ownership, real impact on the product
The process
Initial call
Technical interview - a 45-minute call where we walk through a real technical problem together to understand how you think and reason
Final call
About Rototip.com
Rototip is a digital marketplace connecting companies that need manufactured goods with factories capable of producing them across CNC and other manufacturing technologies. Headquartered in the Netherlands, we're building the platform that makes global manufacturing procurement fast, transparent, and reliable. Rototip is an equal opportunity employer.
