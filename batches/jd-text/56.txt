Your Role Accountabilities...
Engineering and Implementation:
Build and maintain features tightly integrated with our CMS platform for editors and product teams to configure, launch, and monitor A/B and multivariate tests
Collaborate on APIs and services for experimentation, integrations with frontend and backend systems
Write clean, efficient software code with a focus on maintainability and performance
Participate in regular testing, QA coverage analysis, and deployment processes to ensure production readiness
Collaboration and Delivery:
Partner closely with peer engineers to learn and build experimentation systems, from architectural design to iterative feature development to production release
Partner with product and analytics leads to define and deliver high-impact testing features
Participate in sprint planning, sprint reviews, and retrospectives to develop team delivery skills
Contribute to code reviews, architectural design documentation, and engineering process workflows
Mentorship and Growth:
Provide peer mentorship through informal code reviews and collaborative debugging
Share technical insights during team meetings and sprint ceremonies
Lead knowledge-sharing sessions and create technical documentation
Encourage inclusive engineering practices and a test-and-learn mindset
Qualifications and Experience...
Backend Node.js (2+ years of experience)
AWS (Lambda/S3/ECS/RDS) / Infrastructure as code (Terraform preferred)
Unit, Functional Testing & TDD
Typescript / JavaScript
JavaScript frameworks (such as React, Vue, or Angular)
Fluent in English
Bonus Skills:
Kafka
CI/CD
API Development (such as REST or RPC)
Go
Rust

Data engineering
Content Management Systems
