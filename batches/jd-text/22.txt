Joerie van den Dam
Joerie van den Dam
Senior Corporate Recruiter at TicketSwap - We’re hiring 🚀
At TicketSwap, we’re on a mission to be the experience marketplace every fan loves. By bringing fans and experiences together on our marketplace, we enable fans to go to more events they love and support organisers in building lasting success. With over 19 million fans across 42 countries, we bring fans and experiences together - making live events safer, fairer, and more accessible.
As an Android Developer, you’ll play a key role in how fans discover, buy, and experience events on mobile. You’ll work on meaningful product challenges and ship features that reach millions of users around the world.
Ready to help us build the best Android experience for fans everywhere?
What You’ll Do
You’ll join a cross-functional product team where engineers, product managers, and designers work closely together. You’ll take ownership, move fast, and help shape the mobile experience end-to-end.
Your impact:
Build and improve features across our Android app used by millions of fans
Collaborate closely with backend, web, and mobile engineers in a cross-functional squad
Take ownership of features from idea to release and iteration
Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
Contribute to architectural decisions and continuously improve the codebase
Balance speed and quality to deliver value without over-engineering
Work with data and experimentation to improve user experience
Partner with product and design to create intuitive, high-quality user journeys
Share knowledge and learnings within the Android and mobile guild
Stay up to date with new technologies and bring ideas to improve how we work
Contribute to a culture of ownership, curiosity, and continuous improvement
Our tech stack includes:
Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, GraphQL, Firebase, GitHub, Sentry, Lokalise and standard testing frameworks such as Espresso and JUnit. We continuously evolve our stack to stay effective and up to date.
About you
We’re looking for someone who enjoys building great products and takes pride in their craft.
You have solid experience building Android applications with Kotlin
You are pragmatic, but you know the theory too
You know when to move fast and when to focus on quality and detail
You enjoy solving real user problems with simple, thoughtful solutions
You’re comfortable working in cross-functional teams (Agile/Scrum)
You communicate clearly and collaborate easily with non-engineers
You’re curious and open to learning — both technically and on the product side
You take ownership and like seeing your work make a real impact
Experience with A/B testing, experimentation, or analytics tools is a plus
You use AI in your everyday job, or you used it on some personal project
Interest in product metrics or growth is a bonus
Why You’ll Love It Here
You’ll be part of a team that cares deeply about the product, each other, and the fans we serve. We move fast, give ownership, and trust people to do their best work.
You’ll have the freedom to shape the product, learn continuously, and see your work used by millions of people around the world.
Practical Details
Location: Amsterdam (hybrid setup)
Full-time role
No relocation support provided
This role sits within Function Group 6 or 7, depending on experience and seniority level. We’re open to considering both mid-level and senior Android Developers.
TicketSwap is looking for the best and motivated talent out there no matter your nationality, gender, sexual preference or religious beliefs. 🚀
Research shows that women and those in traditionally underrepresented groups tend to apply only if they check all the boxes. That’s a shame, as we want to build a diverse and inclusive team. If you know you’ve got what it takes but don’t quite meet every requirement for the role, we still encourage you to apply.
Apply now – we'll take a look and get back to you as soon as possible! 💥
