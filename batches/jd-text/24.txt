Op het hoofdkantoor
Bij Albert Heijn werken is niet zomaar een baan. Het is werk met betekenis. Je werkt in een informele cultuur samen met een bevlogen team dat graag voorop wil blijven lopen en verschil wil maken. Met jouw unieke talent maak je impact op het leven van al onze klanten. Je leert van experts in hun vakgebied en we helpen je jouw loopbaan vorm te geven. Je kunt bij ons flexibel werken, zowel vanuit huis als op ons hoofdkantoor in Zaandam. Een baan bij Albert Heijn is niet alleen een baan voor nu, maar ook voor later
Wat betekent dit voor jou?
Help build the integration backbone powering warehouse mechanization. As our new Backend Engineer, you will design and develop services that connect WMS, integration platforms, and mechanized systems (WCS), enabling reliable and scalable data exchange across the supply chain.
Operating in a distributed and event-driven environment, you will work on systems that handle real-time flows of orders, stock, and operational events. Your contributions will be key to ensuring data consistency, system resilience, and the smooth coordination between digital systems and physical warehouse operations.
Build systems that matter at real scale. In this role, you will design and build backend services that support business‑critical processes used daily across the organization. Your work directly impacts system reliability, developer productivity, and the ability of teams to deliver features faster and more safely. You will work on production systems at scale, where performance, resilience, and maintainability are first‑class concerns—not afterthoughts.
This Is How You’ll Reinvent Food Retail.
You design, develop, and operate backend services using modern engineering practices
You work with technologies such as Kotlin, event‑driven architectures with Kafka
You take ownership of services end‑to‑end: from design and implementation to production support and continuous improvement, DevOps
You collaborate closely with product, platform, and other engineering teams to make pragmatic technical decisions
How We Work.
Engineers have real ownership and autonomy in technical decisions within our architectural guiderails
With the latest DevAI Tooling we supercharge our development workflow, automate where we can, giving you more time to focus on creative problem-solving.
Code quality matters: peer reviews, clear architecture, and readable code are part of daily work
Technical debt is acknowledged and addressed—not ignored
We value simple, robust solutions over unnecessary complexity
Why this role is interesting for engineers.
You work on systems with real users and real impact
You face non‑trivial engineering challenges around scalability, data flow, and reliability
You have time and space to improve both the codebase and the way we work
You grow technically through exposure to complex systems, experienced engineers, and meaningful ownership
Your new team and office.
You work at the heart of the Albert Heijn supply chain, we are responsible for the on-time delivery of our products at 1200+ Albert Heijn stores every day across the Netherlands and Belgium. Our mission is to provide the best shopping experience to our customers, offering high service levels and fresh products at an affordable price, a task you won't take on solo but as a full-fledged member of the Supply Chain Transport engineering team.
Together with your full stack team you will work on inspiring projects to accelerate our transition into a hybrid Food & Tech company. We want to leverage data and technology to future-proof food shopping and give our customers accessibility to healthy food more easily available for everyone. At Albert Heijn, you team up with inspiring peers in the supply chain tech domain. A community of diverse individuals, working together for our logistic and transport business.
