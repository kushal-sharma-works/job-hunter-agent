Java Software Developer (Active Sports)
At MYLAPS, performance is everything. We support athletes and events in creating their ultimate sports experience. By developing accurate, reliable, and easy-to-use technology we turn data into insights, progress, and fun.
The role
Whether it’s a triathlon, cycling race, or a multi-sport event, as a Java Software Developer you are building our advanced timing solutions which ensures every athlete’s performance is accurately captured.
From chip timing to live results and capturing the closest of finishes with a high level of accuracy our professional event timing software shows sporters most accurate timing results.
What You'll Do
Our Software Development team works on both motorized (Speedhive) as Active Sports (Sporthive) Apps. You will be responsible for the backend part that services these apps for our Active Sports applications.
In this multidisciplinary team you will find colleagues with specializations in IOS, Android and Web development, as well as a QA Engineer working closely together with our Product Owner. As a team we are continuously working on the maintenance of the apps, but also working on new feature implementations and development.
Since we like to understand what our software means to sporters, this position offers the chance to visit small and large scale sporting events to fully understand the accuracy and meaning of our software.
What You Bring
🎯 4+ Years of Software Development experience with Java and preferably Springboot.
🎯 Front-end Development experience in Vue.js is seen as a plus.
🗣 Strong (technical) communication skills in English (Dutch is a plus).
👌Passion for technology and sport.
👌 Cloud experience, preferably Azure, is a bonus.
What We Offer
✨Work with the global market leader in sports timing;
✨A competitive salary and solid benefits package;
✨ 27 vacation days per year;
✨ Pension plan and contribution to health insurance;
✨ Attractive discount on Garmin products;
✨ A dynamic, international workplace with an open and informal culture;
✨ Thorough onboarding with expert training from both technical and commercial teams;
✨ A diverse, inclusive workplace that values different perspectives and backgrounds;
✨ Easy to reach by car, bike, or train.

About MYLAPS Sports Technology
MYLAPS International Headquarters is based in Haarlem, The Netherlands. With offices in The Netherlands, The United States, Australia, Malaysia, Japan and China we support racers, athletes, and events in creating their ultimate sports experience. Every year we capture the performance of over 20 million people all over the world. We develop accurate, reliable, and easy-to-use technology to turn data into insights, progress, and fun. Founded in 1982, we have revolutionized the world of sports timing with groundbreaking innovations and set the standard ever since. Our products and platforms are used at professional events like the Olympics and INDYCAR as well as countless local events around the corner. MYLAPS is a carbon neutral company where we have the responsibility to contribute to a better world.
