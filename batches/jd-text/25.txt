As an Android Developer at Realworks you will work on two apps that are used daily: the Realworks app by more than 18,000 real estate agents (B2B) and the Move.nl app by over 100,000 consumers (B2C). This means our apps always need to perform optimally. Within the Mobile team at Realworks, you get the chance to work on solid apps with many possibilities and direct impact!
Our users are not only real estate agents who want to manage their work quickly and efficiently while on the go, but also consumers who are buying or selling a home. Real estate agents use the Realworks app, for example, to place a new property online with photos and floor plans or to quickly search through current listings. Consumers use the Move.nl app to gain insight into their own property or a property they have purchased. The main reason to join our Mobile team is to show your amazing skills to improve both apps and their functionalities, while ensuring they remain user-friendly.
Your team
You will work together with 2 Android Developers, 4 iOS Developers, and a Product Owner. Together, you ensure the apps run smoothly, and that new features are rolled out seamlessly. We work hard, but we also value having fun: grabbing a drink together, taking a lunch walk, or joining events like Dam tot Dam is part of the culture.
Job requirements
There is no fixed set of skills required to become the best Android Developer at Realworks, but you are familiar with the following tech stack:
Strong knowlegde of Kotlin;
Ability to work with Java code;
DI: Dagger 2 and Hilt;
Strong knowledge of Rest API
Basic knowledge of GraphQI
Strong understanding of MVVM and ability to work with Clean Architecture;
Android SDK and Android Jetpack(DataBinding, Compose, Room);
Ability to work with RxJava2;
Espresso and Junit.
Additionally, you are:
Having a bachelor degree;
Results-oriented;
Curious and eager to learn;
Communicative;
Problem-solving;
Proficient in English and preferably Dutch;
Open to exploring new technologies.
What we offer:
At Realworks we offer a lot of space for your own initiatives and personal development. We are located in a nice office in the city centre of Amsterdam, easy to reach by public transportation and car. Together with 100+ colleagues, we work in small teams, which gives you a lot of responsibility.
When you apply and join us, we offer you:
Balance and Bliss: Achieve work-life harmony and well-being;
Global Vibes: Embrace an international, energetic, and flexible work environment;
Central and Cool Workspace: Enjoy the best of both worlds with our cool, centrally located office with easy accessibility, making your daily commute a breeze;
Fuel for Success: Indulge in healthy lunches when working on-site;
Flexible Work: Embrace hybrid work and travel allowances;
Dutch Courses: Learn the language and dive into the local culture;
Fun-filled Meetups: Engage in quarterly events with activities and drinks;
Empowered Impact: Make a difference and bring your ideas to the self-managed teams;
Ongoing Growth: Fuel your professional development with Realworks' annual learning and development budget;
25 Days Holiday: Relax, recharge, and spend quality time with loved ones with 25 days of annual leave;
Pension Plan: Secure your future with our retirement savings support;
Equipment: Excel in your role with company-provided laptop (MacBook or Windows) and essential tools.
How does our process look like?

Congratulations! You are one step closer to an exciting opportunity.
