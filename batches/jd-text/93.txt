EMEA Legal Intern
At Palo Alto Networks®, we’re united by a shared mission—to protect our digital way of life.
Job Summary
The Role: Inclusion in Action
We are looking for an EMEA Legal Intern to join our Legal team for a 4-month immersive professional journey. This is a hands-on position designed to support our EMEA operations.
You will work on AI integration, contract negotiation strategy, and stakeholder management.
Your Impact (Responsibilities)
Under the supervision of senior legal mentors, you will:
Support the Legal Ops team in the roll-out and daily management of AI-driven legal tools, ensuring maximum efficiency in document review and research.
Assist in the continuous improvement of negotiation playbooks, updating clauses and strategies to reflect the evolving tech-legal landscape.
Draft and update critical job-aids, guidance notes, and internal FAQs that support sales and engineering teams.
Act as a liaison between Legal and internal stakeholders, ensuring clear communication and timely project delivery.
Identify friction points in current legal processes and propose creative, scalable solutions.
Qualifications
What You’ll Bring
Currently enrolled in a final-year Law program (LLB/LLM) or a recent graduate looking for a first major corporate opportunity.
A genuine interest in how AI and automation are transforming the legal profession.
Exceptional written and verbal communication skills (English is our primary language; additional European languages are a plus).
You aren’t afraid to ask “Why?” and thrive in a fast-paced environment where the status quo is constantly being challenged.
You have strong attention to detail and business awareness.
Please note that this role is not eligible for immigration sponsorship, and work visa sponsorship is not available for this position.
