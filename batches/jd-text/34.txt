“The entire customer journey. One platform”
What We Value
Every CM.com colleague, connects to our
4 shared values
:
Drive and Lead
You take initiative, make decisions and recognize opportunities. Making mistakes is part of moving forward.
Grow and Learn
The growth of our colleagues and our customers is our priority. You like to experiment, learn continuously and actively share knowledge and insights with others.
Together and Care
We work together and we believe that diverse perspectives lead to stronger outcomes. You value the social side of work, you show genuine interest and care about one another’s well-being.

By welcoming AI Agents into our organisation, we fundamentally change how work is done. You are comfortable with change and you love working in a high paced environment.
The Role
As a
Backend Developer
you will take ownership of a vital component at the core of CM.com’s product ecosystem. The CDP serves as the backbone for customer data management, enabling clients to store, structure, and utilize their data effectively. It powers key CM.com solutions like marketing software, customer service tools, and AI-driven platforms such as HALO, making it indispensable for many high-profile customers. You’ll play a vital role in evolving this platform. Your work will directly impact how businesses leverage data to create personalized, data-driven experiences.
You will:
Design and implement new features for both the Customer Data Platform and its newly launched successor, the Customer Context Platform. Richer, smarter, and built for a world where AI only delivers value with the right context at the right time.
Build and optimize data pipelines that handle complex customer data at scale.
Ensure scalability, reliability, and top-tier performance across both platforms.
Work closely in a cross-functional Scrum team (five developers, a Product Owner, and a UX Designer), where you’ll have real influence over technical decisions and the platform’s direction.
Use AI tools actively to improve your own work: experiment, automate, and share what works with your team.
Experienced in C#, Web APIs, and microservices, with a solid grasp of backend architecture and relational databases like PostgreSQL.
Comfortable building and optimizing data pipelines. Experience with graph databases or large-scale data processing is a plus.
Familiar with cloud technologies like Google Cloud (BigQuery, Bigtable, Pub/Sub) and infrastructure tooling like Terraform.
Knowledgeable in Kubernetes, Linux/Windows environments, and system monitoring.
Thorough when it comes to testing. You write unit and integration tests as a matter of course, not as an afterthought.
Fluent in English (Dutch is a plus) and based within commuting distance of our Breda office.
