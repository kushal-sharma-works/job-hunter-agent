Direct message the job poster from Levy Professionals
Leonid Kruglikov
Leonid Kruglikov
Delivery Consultant – Cybersecurity, Cloud & Infrastructure at Levy Professionals
We are looking for…
A highly skilled Senior Frontend Developer to join a DevOps-oriented team within our client's internal-facing division. We need a collaborative, autonomous professional who is passionate about continuous learning and high-quality software delivery. You will be a key player in a team of developers helping to manage a complex ecosystem of applications and APIs.
You will:
Develop, maintain, and refactor mission-critical frontend applications using Vue.js (Vue3) and Web Components.
Lead the migration effort from JavaScript to TypeScript to enhance code quality and maintainability.
Ensure high levels of reusability across all applications and component libraries.
Collaborate within a Scrum/Agile environment, working closely with designers, backend engineers, and Product Owners.
Provide guidance and mentorship to junior and mid-level colleagues, driving the team's technical maturity.
Responsibilities
Full-lifecycle participation: from requirement refinement and technical design to delivery and incident support.
Utilizing Azure DevOps for version control and managing CI/CD pipelines.
Contributing to the client's internal knowledge sharing through presentations and hackathons.
Implementing best practices in CSS3, SCSS, and HTML5 to ensure responsive and accessible interfaces.
Who are you?
Experience
Minimum of 5 years of professional experience in frontend development.
Deep expertise in Vue.js (specifically Vue3) and the modern JavaScript ecosystem.
Proven track record in Scrum/Agile environments and DevOps practices (CI/CD, Git).
Experience with large-scale component libraries or Server-Driven UI is highly preferred.
Background in financial services, particularly in payment initiation or online banking, is a significant plus.
Profile
Education:
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
Technical Stack:
Proficient in Vue.js, TypeScript, Lit (Web Components), and Azure Cloud.
Soft Skills:
Strong communicator, mentor-minded, and a proactive problem solver who thrives in a collaborative, autonomous environment.
Tools:
Familiarity with Figma, Vitest, and PrimeView/Emerald is preferred.
About Levy Professionals
Since 2000 we provide professional solutions to organizations ranging from tech start-ups to global players. From our offices in Amsterdam and London we have built an international and local network of skilled employed professionals and contractors fuelled by our passion for connecting skills with projects. Over the years we have fulfilled over 1700 requirements and nowadays we consistently have 250+ professionals recruited and relocated from 14 countries allocated to various projects. Our strength is the way that we see and treat people. This will always be a key factor in our strategy for many years to come.
